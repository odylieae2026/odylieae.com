Instruction Sheet

Become the first player to successfully reach the Palace while carefully managing your water supply. Water Tokens are both your currency and your survival resource. Spend wisely and conserve water throughout the journey.

  • 1 Game Board
  • 1 Dice
  • Water Tokens
  • Property Cards
  • Challenge Cards
  • Water Crisis Cards
  • Positive Action Cards
  • Hourglass
  • Player Tokens (2–6 Players)
  1. Place every player at START.
  2. Give each player 20 Water Tokens.
  3. Shuffle all cards.
  4. Place cards beside the board.
  5. Highest dice roll starts first.

Players buy properties, collect rent, complete challenges, gain or lose water tokens and prepare for the final journey.
