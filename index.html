Why We Created THE LAST DROP

Malaysia has experienced increasing water shortages and water pollution over the years. Many people are still unaware of how valuable clean water is and how everyday actions can affect our water resources.

THE LAST DROP was created to raise awareness about water conservation through an interactive and enjoyable learning experience.

Instead of simply reading about environmental issues, players learn the importance of saving water by making decisions, managing limited resources, and overcoming real-life water challenges throughout the game.

Our goal is to inspire players to remember one simple message:

Every Drop Counts.

"We cannot create more water, but together we can protect every drop."

THE LAST DROP

Instruction Sheet

A Suspense + Strategy Educational Board Game

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Become the first player to successfully reach the Palace while carefully managing your Water Tokens.

Water Tokens are your most valuable resource—they serve as both your currency and your survival supply. Spend wisely, conserve whenever possible, and overcome water-related challenges along the journey.

🎲 GAME COMPONENTS

  • 1 Game Board
  • 1 Dice
  • Water Tokens
  • Property Ownership Cards (or markers)
  • Challenge Cards (Blue)
  • Player Tokens (2–4 players)
  • 30-second timer

⚙️ SET UP

  1. Place all players on the START space.
  2. Give each player 20 Water Tokens (or any amount agreed before the game begins).
  3. Shuffle the Blue Challenge Cards and place them beside the board.
  4. Decide who goes first by rolling the dice. The player with the highest number goes first.

🎮 GAMEPLAY

The game has two phases.

🏜️ PHASE 1 – RESOURCE BUILDING

(Rounds 1–2)

Players take turns rolling the dice, moving around the board, and following the instructions on the space they land on.

During this phase, players can:

  • Buy properties.
  • Collect rent.
  • Gain Water Tokens.
  • Lose Water Tokens.
  • Complete challenge questions.
  • Save Water Tokens for the final journey.

The first two rounds allow players to build resources before the race to the Palace begins.

🗺️ BOARD SPACES

🟨 Yellow – Property

If the property is unowned:

  • You may purchase it using Water Tokens.

If the property is owned:

  • Pay the required Water Token rent to the owner.

Players may sell or trade properties if all players agree.

🟥 Red – Water Crisis Space

When you land on a red space, follow the instruction printed on the board.

Examples include:

  • Lose Water Tokens.
  • Move backwards.
  • Skip a turn.

These spaces represent real-life water issues such as pollution, drought, and water shortages.

🟩 Green – Positive Action Space

When you land on a green space, follow the instruction printed on the board.

Examples include:

  • Gain Water Tokens.
  • Move forward.
  • Receive other rewards.

These spaces reward good environmental practices and water conservation.

🟦 Blue – Solution Challenge

Draw a Blue Challenge Card and answer the question.

You have 30 seconds to answer.

  • Correct answer: Gain 2 Water Tokens.
  • Incorrect answer: Lose 2 Water Tokens.

💧 WATER TOKENS

Water Tokens are used to:

  • Buy properties.
  • Pay rent.
  • Pay penalties.
  • Continue your journey.

Manage your Water Tokens carefully because they will also be needed during the final stage of the game.

🏰 PHASE 2 – JOURNEY TO THE PALACE

After two complete rounds, the final competition begins.

Players continue moving around the outside of the board as normal.

Whenever a player lands exactly on the Journey to Palace space, they immediately begin travelling along the Palace Path.

🎲 MOVING INSIDE THE PALACE PATH

Roll the dice normally.

Move according to the number rolled.

To win, you must land exactly on the Palace.

📖 Example

You are 2 spaces away from the Palace.

You roll 5.

Move:

  • 2 spaces forward to the Palace.
  • Then use the remaining 3 spaces moving backwards.

You must wait until a future turn to land exactly on the Palace.

💧 WATER SYMBOL SPACES

While travelling along the Palace Path, if you land on a Water Symbol space, draw a Blue Challenge Card and answer the question.

You have 30 seconds to answer.

  • Correct answer: Gain 2 Water Tokens and continue the game.
  • Incorrect answer: Skip your next turn.

This rule only applies while travelling on the Palace Path during Phase 2.

💰 BANKRUPTCY RULE

If a player becomes bankrupt (0 Water Tokens), they are not eliminated.

They must recover by choosing one of the following:

  • Sell a property back to the bank to gain Water Tokens.
  • Draw a Blue Challenge Card and answer it.
  • Correct answer: Gain 2 Water Tokens.
  • Incorrect answer: Lose 2 Water Tokens (no penalty if already at 0).

The player may continue playing once they regain Water Tokens.

💧 RUNNING OUT OF WATER

If you run out of Water Tokens while travelling on the Palace Path, you must stop moving until you gain more Water Tokens.

No player is eliminated from the game.

🏆 WINNING THE GAME

The first player who:

  • Successfully reaches the Palace,
  • Lands exactly on the final Palace space,
  • Has enough Water Tokens to complete the journey,

is declared the winner.

📜 IMPORTANT RULES

✔ Water Tokens are both money and survival resources.

✔ Players may only enter the Palace Path by landing exactly on the Journey to Palace space.

✔ You must land exactly on the Palace to win.

✔ If your dice roll is higher than the remaining spaces to the Palace, move to the Palace first, then use the remaining spaces moving backwards.

✔ Landing on a Water Symbol during Phase 2 requires you to answer a Blue Challenge Card.

✔ Every Blue Challenge Card follows the same rule:

  • 30 seconds to answer.
  • Correct answer: Gain 2 Water Tokens.
  • Incorrect answer: Lose 2 Water Tokens.

✔ Bankruptcy players must recover by selling property or answering a Blue Challenge Card.

💧 EDUCATIONAL MESSAGE

Every Drop Counts!

The Last Drop teaches players that water is a precious resource. Through strategy, resource management, and environmental challenges, players learn how everyday actions can help conserve water and reduce the impact of Malaysia's water crisis.

Think wisely. Spend carefully. Save water. Reach the Palace before the last drop is gone!
