Why We Created THE LAST DROP

Malaysia has experienced increasing water shortages and water pollution over the years. Many people are still unaware of how valuable clean water is and how everyday actions can affect our water resources.

THE LAST DROP was created to raise awareness about water conservation through an interactive and enjoyable learning experience.

Instead of simply reading about environmental issues, players learn the importance of saving water by making decisions, managing limited resources, and overcoming real-life water challenges throughout the game.

Our goal is to inspire players to remember one simple message:

Every Drop Counts.

"We cannot create more water, but together we can protect every drop."

THE LAST DROP

Instruction Sheet

A Suspense + Strategy Educational Board Game

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Become the first player to successfully reach the Palace while carefully managing your water supply.

Water tokens are your most valuable resource—they are both your currency and your survival supply. Spend wisely, conserve whenever possible, and overcome water-related challenges along the journey.

🎲 GAME COMPONENTS

  • 1 Game Board
  • 1 Dice
  • Water Tokens
  • Property Ownership Cards (or markers)
  • Challenge Cards (Blue)
  • Water Crisis Cards (Red)
  • Positive Action Cards (Green)
  • 1 Hourglass (30 or 60 seconds)
  • Player Tokens (2–6 players)

⚙️ SET UP

  1. Place all players on the START space.
  2. Give each player
    • 20 Water Tokens (or any amount decided before the game begins.)
  3. Shuffle the
    • Challenge Cards
    • Water Crisis Cards
    • Positive Action Cards
  4. Place the cards beside the board.
  5. Decide who goes first by rolling the dice. The highest number starts.

🎮 GAMEPLAY

The game has two phases.

🏜️ PHASE 1 – RESOURCE BUILDING

(Rounds 1–2)

Players take turns moving around the board just like a Monopoly game.

On each turn:

  1. Roll the dice.
  2. Move your token.
  3. Follow the instruction on the space you land on.

Players may:

  • Buy properties.
  • Collect rent.
  • Gain water.
  • Lose water.
  • Complete challenges.
  • Save water for the final journey.

The first two rounds are used to prepare for the final competition.

🗺️ BOARD SPACES

🟨 Yellow – Property

If the property is unowned:

  • You may purchase it using water tokens.

If the property is owned:

  • Pay the required water token rent to the owner.

Players may sell or trade properties if everyone agrees.

🟥 Red – Water Crisis

Draw a Water Crisis card and follow the instructions.

Examples:

  • Lose water tokens.
  • Move backwards.
  • Skip a turn.

These represent real-life water problems such as pollution and drought.

🟩 Green – Positive Action

Draw a Positive Action card.

You may:

  • Gain water tokens.
  • Move forward.
  • Receive other rewards.

These cards reward good environmental practices.

🟦 Solution Challenge

Players must answer the challenge question before the hourglass runs out. Questions answered correctly will be rewarded with 2 Water Tokens. Questions answered incorrectly will require the player to pay 2 Water Tokens as a penalty. Think carefully before answering!

💧 WATER TOKENS

Water tokens are used to:

  • Buy properties
  • Pay rent
  • Pay penalties
  • Continue your journey

If your water tokens become very low, spend carefully because you will need them during the final stage.

🏰 PHASE 2 – JOURNEY TO THE PALACE

After two complete rounds, the real competition begins.

Players continue moving around the outside board as normal.

Whenever a player lands exactly on the Journey to Palace space, they immediately begin travelling along the palace path.

🎲 MOVING INSIDE THE PALACE PATH

Roll the dice normally.

Move according to the number rolled.

To win, you must land exactly on the Palace.

📖 Example

You are 2 spaces away from the Palace.

You roll 5.

Move:

  • 2 spaces forward to the Palace.
  • Then continue the remaining 3 spaces backwards.

You must wait until a future turn to land exactly on the Palace.

💧 RUNNING OUT OF WATER

If you run out of Water Tokens while travelling through the Palace Path:

You must:

  1. Draw a Blue Challenge Card.
  2. Answer within one hourglass.

✅ If Correct

  • Receive 3 Emergency Water Tokens (or another amount decided by the teacher/game master).
  • Continue your journey.

❌ If Incorrect

  • Stay where you are.
  • Wait until your next turn to try again.

No player is eliminated from the game.

🏆 WINNING THE GAME

The first player who:

  • Successfully reaches the Palace,
  • Lands exactly on the final Palace space,
  • And has enough water to complete the journey,

is declared the winner.

📜 IMPORTANT RULES

✔ Water Tokens are both money and survival resources.

✔ Players may only enter the Palace Path by landing exactly on the Journey to Palace space.

✔ You must land exactly on the Palace to win.

✔ If your dice roll is too high, move to the Palace first, then use the remaining steps moving backwards.

✔ Players cannot continue travelling through the Palace Path without water.

✔ The hourglass must be used for every Blue Challenge Card.

💧 EDUCATIONAL MESSAGE

Every Drop Counts!

The Last Drop teaches players that water is a precious resource. Through strategy, decision-making, and environmental challenges, players learn how everyday actions can help conserve water and reduce the impact of Malaysia's water crisis.

Think wisely. Spend carefully. Save water. Reach the Palace before the last drop is gone!
