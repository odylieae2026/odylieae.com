Instruction Sheet

Welcome to THE LAST DROP, an educational strategy board game inspired by Malaysia's water crisis. Every decision you make affects your survival. Manage your water wisely, overcome challenges, and become the first player to reach the Palace.

🎯 Objective

Become the first player to reach the Palace while carefully managing your Water Tokens. Water Tokens are both your currency and your survival resource throughout the game.

🎲 Game Components

  • 1 Game Board
  • 1 Dice
  • Water Tokens
  • Property Ownership Cards
  • Challenge Cards (Blue)
  • Water Crisis Cards (Red)
  • Positive Action Cards (Green)
  • 1 Hourglass (30–60 seconds)
  • Player Tokens (2–6 Players)

⚙️ Game Setup

  1. Place every player at the START space.
  2. Give each player 20 Water Tokens.
  3. Shuffle all Challenge, Water Crisis and Positive Action cards.
  4. Place all cards beside the board.
  5. The player with the highest dice roll starts first.

🎮 Gameplay

The game consists of two phases. During the first two rounds, players move around the board, buy properties, collect rent, complete challenges and manage their Water Tokens. After the preparation phase, players begin the final journey towards the Palace.

🏜️ Board Spaces

  • 🟨 Yellow – Property
    Purchase unowned properties or pay rent to the owner.
  • 🟥 Red – Water Crisis
    Draw a Water Crisis Card and follow its instructions.
  • 🟩 Green – Positive Action
    Draw a Positive Action Card to receive rewards.
  • 🟦 Blue – Solution Challenge
    Answer a sustainability question before the hourglass runs out.

💧 Water Tokens

Water Tokens are the most valuable resource in the game. They are used to purchase properties, pay rent, pay penalties and continue travelling through the Palace Journey. Spend carefully because every drop matters.

🏰 Journey to the Palace

After two complete rounds, players who land exactly on the Journey to Palace space may enter the Palace Path. Players must land exactly on the final Palace space to win. If the dice roll is too high, move to the Palace first and use the remaining steps by moving backwards.

🚰 Running Out of Water

Players who run out of Water Tokens during the Palace Journey must answer a Blue Challenge Card. A correct answer earns Emergency Water Tokens and allows the journey to continue. An incorrect answer means waiting until the next turn. No player is eliminated from the game.

🏆 Winning the Game

The winner is the first player who reaches the Palace exactly while having enough Water Tokens to complete the journey.

📖 Educational Message

Every Drop Counts.

THE LAST DROP encourages players to understand the importance of water conservation through strategic thinking, teamwork and environmental awareness. Remember: Think Wisely. Spend Carefully. Save Water.
